Why Foodmates?

Overt Objectives
A mobile app for householders to plan their meals in advance on a daily/ weekly basis, have groceries delivered to their door, manage their pantry, and give the Domestic Kitchen Maid clear, hassle-free recipe instructions.
Covert Objectives
  • To keep customers and increase lifetime value, become a permanent part of the customer's home cooking ecosystem.
  • To establish private label brands for kitchen ingredients.
  • To gain a thorough understanding of the customer's food and taste preferences in order to create offers that are tailored to their needs.

Designing interface for Kitchen maids
Ergonomics of Kitchen maids
  • Kitchen maids are illiterate in India
  • They have no understanding of technology
  • They have never used a smartphone/tablet
  • They live in cities where around ten languages are spoken
  • They have no idea how to operate kitchen equipment
  • They are eager to try & learn new recipes.
The challenges
  • Kitchen maids must be able to understand user interfaces
  • They must know what clickables, draggables, and swippables are
  • They must know where something will go when they click it
  • They should not have to remember things
  • They should not have to read the interface much
  • They should understand labels and icons
  • They should be able to navigate the flows without getting lost/confused
Behaviours & Pain points
Tablet Interface for Kitchen maids
Intelligent recipe instructor

Cooking maids usually prepare several dishes at the same time by managing idle time such as baking, boiling, and frying. This process should be adopted by our system, and the instructions should be displayed accordingly.

Timely instruction for advanced preparation

We also had to factor in the fact that some dishes require advance preparation. Chole, for example, must be soaked overnight before being used in any dish. At the end of the current cooking session, our system should continue to give instructions for advanced preparation for the next cooking session.

Voice memo from Householder

Householders may wish to inform the kitchen maids of any allergies or other concerns they may have. The option for householders to send voice memos to the maids had been enabled.
