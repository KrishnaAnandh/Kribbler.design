My Principles

Start with Why, End with How

Before I do anything, I always question myself, "Why am I doing this?" Something will be meaningless or worthless if you don't know why it's being done.

Key to success isn’t best UI

The ultimate cause for a business's success would be the best User Experience and Customer Experience, not just how nice the UI looks.

Ownership & Craftmanship

When I get into a project, I take ownership of it and immerse myself in it in order to provide excellent craftsmanship, regardless of how much it pays me back.

Design is for everyone

I design with everyone in mind, despite the fact that my primary audience is a small group and not ignoring the remaining audience

Know the technology deeper

Knowing the technology of what they do, whether it's a professional or artistic career, helps them achieve the finest work possible.

Communication is the key

Communication is necessary for the transmission of information and ideas. Rather than assumptions, effective communication leads to effective outcomes.
